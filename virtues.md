# Virtues

These virtues are tracked daily as simple, binary reflections.
The goal is awareness, honesty, and steady formation — not perfection.

---

## Silence
**Speak only when it benefits; avoid trifling conversation.**  
Guard your tongue. Let speech be intentional, truthful, and necessary. Avoid fillers and flattery; speak slow and pause before response. Even the fool seems perceptive when he remains quiet. 

---

## Frugality
**Make no expense but to do good to others or yourself; waste nothing.**  
Use resources wisely. Spend with purpose, not impulse.

---

## Industry
**Lose no time; be always employ'd in something useful; cut off all unnecessary actions.**  
Work diligently. Avoid idleness and distractions that erode focus.

---

## Humility
**Think rightly of yourself; neither inflating nor diminishing your worth.**  
Receive correction gladly. Serve without seeking recognition.

---

## Mindfulness
**Be present and attentive to the moment before you.**  
Act deliberately rather than reactively. Notice thoughts, actions, and motives. Observe from 3rd-person perspective. 

---

## Contentment
**Be grateful and at peace with what is given today.**  
Resist envy and dissatisfaction. Practice gratitude in all circumstances.

---

## Abstinence
**Exercise self-control over appetites and impulses.**  
Say no to excess. Choose restraint that leads to freedom.

---

## Purity
**Guard your heart, mind, and body.**  
Choose what is clean and life-giving. Reject what contaminates thoughts, desires, or actions.

---

## Wisdom
**Seek understanding and choose rightly.**  
Apply knowledge with discernment. Favor long-term good over short-term ease.

---

## Moderation
**Avoid extremes; maintain balance in all things.**  
Neither indulgence nor deprivation, but steady restraint.

---

## Generosity
**Give liberally as you've been given.**
Freely giving time, attention, resources, and grace without resentment or expectation of return.
